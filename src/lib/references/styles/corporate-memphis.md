# corporate-memphis

Bold flat-vector illustration with vibrant geometric fills, disproportionate human figures, and clean modern composition. Think Slack, Spotify, or Notion illustration style.

## Color Palette

- **Background**: Clean white (#FFFFFF) or very light pastel tint. Can use soft gradient backgrounds for larger sections.
- **Primary**: Vibrant, saturated — electric purple (#6C5CE7), coral orange (#FF6B6B), teal (#00B894), warm yellow (#FDCB6E)
- **Secondary**: Deep navy (#2D3436) for contrast, soft lilac (#A29BFE), mint (#55EFC4)
- **Fills**: Solid flat colors — no gradients within shapes, but gradient backgrounds are OK
- **Accents**: Bright spot colors for data emphasis (hot pink, electric blue)

## Illustration Technique

### Flat Vector Style
- All illustrations rendered as flat vector art — solid color fills, NO outlines (or very thin if needed)
- Shapes are geometric and clean but slightly organic — not perfectly rigid
- Layer overlapping shapes to create depth without shadows
- Use color blocking to define elements (lighter fills for background, saturated for foreground)

### Illustrated Figures
- Human figures with disproportionate limbs (long legs, small heads, large hands)
- Abstract body shapes — not anatomically realistic
- Diverse representation in skin tones, clothing, and poses
- Figures interacting with objects, charts, and abstract elements
- Expressive gestures and dynamic poses

### Data Visualization
- Charts rendered in flat vector style: clean bars, smooth curves, pie slices in brand colors
- Big numbers rendered in bold sans-serif, surrounded by decorative geometric elements
- Trend arrows and indicators in accent colors
- Floating geometric shapes (circles, triangles, abstract forms) as decorative elements around data

### Scene Elements
- Each section gets its own illustrated scene: a figure working at a desk, people collaborating, abstract concept visualization
- Plant and nature accents (potted plants, leaves, organic shapes) soften the geometric feel
- Floating geometric elements (dots, lines, abstract shapes) fill empty areas
- Device screens showing simplified UI/chart mockups

## Typography

- Clean sans-serif throughout (Inter, Poppins, or similar aesthetic)
- Bold headings in dark navy or black
- Body text in medium weight, good contrast
- Minimal decoration — let the illustrations carry the visual weight
- Numbers and statistics rendered LARGE and bold

## Composition

- Clean, airy composition with deliberate whitespace between sections
- Strong color blocking to separate sections
- Illustrations and text on equal footing — neither dominates
- Consistent margin and padding rhythm

## Best For

Business presentations, tech product overviews, SaaS marketing, corporate training, quarterly reports, market analysis, startup pitch decks
