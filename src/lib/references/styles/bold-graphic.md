# bold-graphic

High-contrast comic/graphic novel style with bold outlines, dramatic compositions, and halftone textures. Think magazine cover art meets graphic novel page.

## Color Palette

- **Primary**: Bold primaries — red (#E63946), yellow (#FFD60A), blue (#1D3557), jet black (#000000)
- **Background**: White with halftone dot patterns, or dramatic black with color pops
- **Accents**: Neon highlights (hot pink, electric green) for emphasis
- **Shadows**: Heavy black shadows, no subtle gradients — everything is high contrast
- **Halftone**: Visible dot patterns for mid-tones (Ben-Day dots effect)

## Illustration Technique

### Bold Line Work
- Thick black outlines (3-5px equivalent) on ALL elements — nothing exists without a bold border
- Line weight variation for emphasis: thicker for foreground, slightly thinner for background
- Clean, confident strokes — not sketchy or uncertain
- Dramatic foreshortening and perspective for dynamic compositions

### Comic Panels
- Content sections can be arranged in comic panel borders
- Action lines radiating from focal points for emphasis
- Motion blur and speed lines for dynamic elements
- Dramatic angles — low angle for power, high angle for overview

### Halftone & Texture
- Ben-Day halftone dots for shading and mid-tones
- Cross-hatching for shadows in graphic novel variant
- Flat color fills with halftone gradients
- Screen-print texture overlay for vintage pop-art feel

### Data Visualization
- Charts rendered with bold outlines and flat fill colors
- Big numbers in IMPACT-weight typography, filling their containers
- Trend arrows LARGE and dramatic — not subtle indicators
- Statistical callouts in starburst or explosion shapes
- Comparison data shown with dramatic scale differences

### Illustrated Scenes
- People rendered in graphic novel style: strong features, dramatic poses
- Objects drawn with bold precision and exaggerated proportions
- Scenes composed with dramatic lighting (high contrast, heavy shadows)
- Background elements simplified but present

## Typography

- Comic book lettering: bold, slightly irregular, energetic
- IMPACT or similar heavy sans-serif for headlines
- Sound effect typography (POW, BANG) for pop-art variant emphasis
- Caption boxes with yellow or white backgrounds and bold borders
- All text has strong visual presence — nothing is quietly set

## Composition

- High-energy: elements should feel like they're bursting out of their containers
- Strong diagonal composition creates movement and drama
- Contrast between large and small elements for visual rhythm
- Color used strategically — mostly limited palette with one or two accent pops

## Best For

Marketing materials, attention-grabbing social media, brand announcements, competitive analysis, product launches, high-energy presentations, entertainment content
